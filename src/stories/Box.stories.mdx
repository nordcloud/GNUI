## Props

- `dark`: `boolean` — _optional_
- `minHeight`: `string` — _optional_
- `radius`: `string` — _optional_
- `padding`: `string` — _optional_
- `margin`: `string` — _optional_
- `shadow`: `string` — _optional_

> This exists only to properly display `Snow` component and should be rewritten with `theme`.
